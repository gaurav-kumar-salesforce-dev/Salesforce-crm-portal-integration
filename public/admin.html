Role Hierarchy

Define your organization's data visibility structure. Users in higher roles see records owned by roles below them.

How role hierarchy works: Roles control record visibility, not permissions. A Sales Manager assigned to a role above Sales Reps will automatically see all records owned by those Sales Reps. Roles at the same level cannot see each other's records unless sharing rules are applied. Roles are fully custom — define them to match your data access needs, not your org chart.
